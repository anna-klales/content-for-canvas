In order to get credit for class participation, you'll need to bring a laptop, smartphone, or tablet to every class.

To use Canvas on your smartphone or tablet:

  • Download the free "Canvas by Instructure" app (for iOS or Android)
  • When you log in, you might need to enter the Harvard Canvas domain:

canvas.harvard.edu

The app lets you take in-class quizzes, see your assignments, check grades, and send messages to students or instructors.

Please do this before you come to the first class!
